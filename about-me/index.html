About me

Hailing from the northern wastes of Sweden, the province of Jämtland. Upon starting senior high school, I decided to move down to the south and study the natural sciences, programming and misc. courses such as introduction to nuclear engineering. Realizing that chemical engineering seemed interesting, I moved to Gothenburg to study at Chalmers. After obtaining my B.Sc. in Chemical Engineering, I decided on to continue with a Master in Materials Chemistry rather than delving deeper into topic such as production plant design. desing the chance to combine the topicsof my bachelor and master, I finally pursued a PhD in Materials Science on the topic of heterogeneous catalysis, a degree which will be finished in April 2021.
